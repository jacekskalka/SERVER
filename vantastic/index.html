Welcome to Vintage Vantastic


We specialize in buying, rebuilding and selling Vintage Vans and Mobile Food Trucks.


The business started with our own HY Van, restored by hand and operated by the owner as a mobile food truck. Now Vintage Vantastic has vans and food trucks all over the world. We know the business inside out, because we started at the bottom and worked our way up to the top.


If you are looking to start your own mobile business or you are looking for the ideal way to advertise or market your business then Vintage Vantastic is perfect for you.. Tell us your vision and we'll work closely with you to make sure you get exactly what you need. Like you, we are passionate about what we do.


We provide each and every one of our customers with the best service and the highest quality workmanship possible. We place quality and service first and we want your investment in us to continue making you and your business a success for decades to come.
